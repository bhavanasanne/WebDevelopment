MS DHONI

BIOGRAPHY

FAMILY

MS Dhoni was born on June 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Jharkhand. His father, Narendra Singh and mother, Rajani, were the ones who gave him the name Mahendra Singh Dhoni. His family belongs to a cricketing background.

CAREER

MS Dhoni entered the international cricket scene in 2004 when he played for India against Bangladesh. Later that year, he was chosen for a tour of Zimbabwe and Kenya. In 2006, he made his Test debut against Sri Lanka and ODI debut against West Indies. He has been a regular member since then. MS Dhoni’s performance in the 2007 T20 World Cup brought him to prominence. He was named team captain for the tournament.

RECORDS

MS Dhoni’s records include the most number of matches played by an Indian player in T20 internationals, with 111 appearances. He has also scored 17 half-centuries in T20 internationals.

AWARDS

His achievements with the bat and glove have earned him several awards, including the ICC ODI Player of the Year award twice (2008 & 2009), the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy (ICC Cricketer of the Year) twice (2008 & 2009), the Wisden Leading Cricketer of the World award twice (2009 & 2013), and the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award twice (2008 & 2013).
